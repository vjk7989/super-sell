# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Love Loom serves shoppers browsing fashion and lifestyle products on mobile, tablet, and desktop before ordering through WhatsApp. Users want to discover products quickly, compare categories and brands, inspect pricing, availability, ratings, sizes, colors, materials, and similar items, then send a clear order request without creating an account or using a payment gateway.

## Product Purpose

Love Loom is a premium ecommerce catalogue for clothing and fashion products. It exists to present curated products, categories, and brands with a polished retail feel, while making the buying path simple through configurable WhatsApp order redirection. Success means shoppers can search, filter, view product details, add items to a persistent cart, share products, and send complete product or cart order details to the store on WhatsApp.

## Brand Personality

Clean, premium, professional. The experience should feel modern, fashion-forward, conversion-focused, and trustworthy, with smooth motion, strong hierarchy, and enough editorial polish to avoid feeling like a generic ecommerce template.

## Anti-references

Avoid basic template storefronts, cluttered marketplace pages, childish animation, hard-to-read glass effects, overused decorative blur, weak product hierarchy, hidden buy actions, and scattered hardcoded contact details. Do not add payment, authentication, backend, admin panels, or features that imply full checkout infrastructure before WhatsApp ordering is ready.

## Design Principles

Lead with product confidence: product imagery, price, availability, brand, and order actions should be easy to find on every commerce surface.

Make WhatsApp ordering feel intentional: buying and checkout messages should be complete, clear, configurable, and trustworthy rather than a bolted-on contact link.

Use premium restraint: bento layouts, glass, gradients, glow, and motion should support hierarchy and conversion, not compete with products.

Design mobile-first browsing: search, filters, cart count, product cards, menus, and detail actions should be fast and comfortable on small screens.

Keep commerce data structured: products, categories, brands, site config, cart logic, sharing, formatting, and WhatsApp utilities should remain reusable and strongly typed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible, readable ecommerce defaults: strong text contrast over glass backgrounds, visible focus states, disabled states for out-of-stock items, keyboard-friendly controls, reduced-motion alternatives for animations, responsive layouts without text overflow, and clear labels for cart, share, filter, search, and WhatsApp actions.
